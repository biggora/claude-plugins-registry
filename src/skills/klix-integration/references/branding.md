# Klix Branding & Representation Guidelines

## Logo Requirements

- Show Klix logo alongside other payment methods in website footer and checkout
- Download assets from: https://developers.klix.app/static-assets/

## Available Assets

### Klix Pay Buttons (SVG)
- Card only: `VISA_MC_CtP.svg`
- Card + Apple Pay: `APay_VISA_MC_CtP.svg`
- Card + Apple Pay + Google Pay: `GPay_APay_VISA_MC_CtP.svg`
- Apple Pay standalone: `klix-applepay.svg`
- Google Pay standalone: `klix-googlepay.svg`
- Click to Pay: `click_to_pay.svg`

### Pay Later Logos (SVG + PNG)
- Generic: `icon-klix_pay_later.svg` / `.png`
- Latvia: `logo_LV.svg` / `.png`
- Lithuania: `logo_LT.svg` / `.png`
- Estonia: `logo_EE.svg` / `.png`

## Checkout Button Labels

### Visual Buttons
Download language-specific button assets from the static assets page.

### Text-Only Button Labels

| Scenario | Label |
|----------|-------|
| Card payments | Payment card / Klix |
| Online banking | Online banking |
| Installments | Installments - Klix Pay Later |
| Card + banking | Payment card or online banking - Klix |
| All methods | Payment card / Online banking / installments - Klix |

## Landing Page

Merchants should create a Klix info page for customer questions.
- Use provided Word templates in relevant languages
- PSD banners available for customization
- Changes to text/banners require written approval from marketing@klix.app

## Apple Pay
Follow Apple's official representation guidelines for custom Apple Pay integrations.
